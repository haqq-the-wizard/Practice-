HTML, or Hypertext Markup Language, is the foundation of every webpage. it's used to structure the content and element of a webpage, like headings, paragraphs, images, and links.

CSS, or Cascading Style Sheets, is all about making your webpage look visually appealing. It's used to control the layout, colors, fonts, and overall design of your webpage.

JavaScript is a powerfull scripting language that adds interactinity and functionality to your webpage. It allows you to create dynamics elements, handle user interactions, and perform actions based on certain events.
